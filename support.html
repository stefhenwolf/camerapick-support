CameraPick

Support

CameraPick helps users discover cameras and lenses, compare specs, browse pricing views, and save favorites.

Common Topics

  • Questions about ads or privacy choices
  • Problems restoring the Remove Ads purchase
  • Incorrect camera, lens, spec, image, or pricing data
  • Feature requests, country coverage requests, or bug reports

Before You Contact Support

  • Make sure the app is updated to the latest version
  • Check your selected country and language inside app settings
  • If the issue is purchase-related, try Restore Purchases first
  • If the issue is ad-related, refresh ad consent inside settings
